About Mustard Seed

An Education That
Changes Everything

Mustard Seed International Schools are private yet affordable schools offering a stimulating Cambridge curriculum and a deeply supportive environment — so every student experiences success every single day.

Situated in Kiamunyi, Nakuru, on the scenic Nakuru-Eldoret corridor, our campus is home to a mosaic of cultures, ideas, and ambitions. We believe education is a path, not a destination.

Academic Programmes

Our Schools

From our nurturing Early Years through to Senior School, every stage is designed for success.

Ages 2–6

Early Years Foundation

Our Early Years programme creates a warm, play-based environment that sparks curiosity from the very first day. Based on the EYFS framework, children explore language, numeracy, and creative arts through structured play and guided discovery.

  • Play-based and inquiry-led learning
  • Low student-to-teacher ratios
  • Outdoor discovery garden
  • Music, art, and movement daily
  • Parent partnership programmes
Apply for Early Years
Ages 6–12

Junior School

Building on early foundations, Junior School develops critical thinkers and confident communicators through the Cambridge Primary programme. Students are encouraged to ask questions, collaborate, and lead — skills that last a lifetime.

  • Cambridge Primary (Maths, English, Science)
  • ICT integration across all subjects
  • Drama, music, and art clubs
  • School library & reading programme
  • Inter-school sports competitions
Apply for Junior School
Ages 12–18

Senior School

Senior School unlocks the world's best universities. Students pursue Cambridge IGCSE and A-Level qualifications with expert guidance, university counselling, and leadership opportunities that build the whole person.

  • Cambridge IGCSE & A Levels
  • Dedicated university counsellor
  • Science labs, computer centre & library
  • Leadership, debate, and MUN clubs
  • 97% university progression rate
Apply for Senior School
Weekly & Full Boarding

Flexible Boarding

Our boarding option provides a safe, nurturing home-from-home that complements academic life. Students enjoy supervised study hours, recreational activities, and the 24/7 support of our dedicated pastoral team.

  • Weekly and full boarding available
  • Supervised evening study halls
  • Recreational & fitness facilities
  • Nutritious, balanced meals daily
  • 24/7 pastoral care and security
