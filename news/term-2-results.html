Term 2 Results: A Record-Breaking Year

Published: February 2026

← Back to News

We are incredibly proud to announce the latest academic results for our Senior School students following the completion of Term 2. This year has seen an unprecedented level of success across the board, setting a new benchmark for academic excellence at Mustard Seed International Schools.

Unprecedented Achievement

Over 95% of our students achieved or surpassed their target grades across both the Cambridge IGCSE and A-Level curriculums. This remarkable accomplishment is a testament to the extraordinary dedication, resilience, and hard work of the students.

We are particularly thrilled to highlight the exceptional performances in the STEM subjects and Languages, where the number of A* and A grades saw a 20% increase from the previous academic year. Such achievements not only reflect the students' intellectual capability but also the robust and engaging learning environment fostered by our dedicated teaching staff.

A Collaborative Effort

These record-breaking results do not happen in isolation. They are the result of a powerful partnership between the school, the students, and our incredibly supportive parent community. Our faculty has gone above and beyond, providing personalized tutoring, comprehensive revision sessions, and unwavering pastoral support to ensure every child reached their full potential.

Looking Ahead to the Future

As we celebrate these incredible milestones, we also look forward to what the future holds for these young scholars. These strong academic foundations will undoubtedly open doors to some of the most prestigious universities globally, turning their aspirations into reality.

Congratulations once again to the entire Senior School cohort. You have made yourselves, your families, and your school immensely proud. We cannot wait to see what you achieve next!
