Annual Sports Day Draws Largest Crowd Yet

Published: January 2026

← Back to News

The energy across the Mustard Seed campus was electric this past Friday as we hosted our highly anticipated Annual Sports Day. It was a spectacular display of athleticism, teamwork, and school spirit that brought together our largest ever crowd of students, parents, staff and esteemed alumni.

A Day of Thrilling Competition

From the early morning track events to the intense tug-of-war finale, the competition was fierce but friendly. Our four school houses — Lions, Eagles, Panthers, and Sharks — battled it out across a diverse range of athletic challenges.

Highlights of the day included record-shattering performances in the upper school 100m sprint, an incredibly close finish in the primary school relay races, and the ever-popular parent-teacher obstacle course that drew roaring applause and laughter from the stands.

More Than Just Winning

"While celebrating our sporting champions is fantastic, the true victory lies in the sportsmanship and camaraderie demonstrated by all our students today," remarked our Head of Athletics. Indeed, the sight of older students cheering on the younger ones, and competitors embracing one another at the finish line, perfectly encapsulated the core values of Mustard Seed International Schools.

Community Spirit in Action

The event was masterfully organized by our Senior School student council, who also managed the refreshment stalls raising funds for our local community outreach program. We want to extend our deepest gratitude to all the parents and families who came to loud support and make the day such an unforgettable success.
